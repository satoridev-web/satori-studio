# SATORI Product Delivery Principles

## Core Principles

- Keep the Lite foundation stable and operationally predictable.
- Prioritise governance, maintainability, and release safety.
- Separate open-core stewardship from commercial feature packaging.
- Avoid regressions in the underlying builder runtime.

## Licensing & Sustainability

### SATORI Studio – Lite
- Free
- GPL-compliant
- Transparent fork attribution

### SATORI Studio – Pro
- Commercial licensing
- Tiered pricing:
  - Single Site
  - 5 Site
  - Agency
- No lifetime unlimited model (to preserve sustainability)

## Release Discipline

- Documentation, architecture, and delivery messaging must remain aligned.
- Lite and Pro responsibilities must remain explicit in all planning and release materials.
